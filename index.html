Jie Wang,
School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology

Biography

I am an associate professor in the school of Cyber Science and Engineering of Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I obtained my PhD degree in June 2021 from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and was fortunately supervised by Prof. Kun Sun and Prof. Yuewu Wang. During my Ph.D. career, I worked as a visiting scholar and studied a lot from Prof. Kun Sun at George Mason University.

Research interest

My research interests mainly focus on Software and System Security, including Trusted Computing System, Kernel Security and Side-channel Attack.
